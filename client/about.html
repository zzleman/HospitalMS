About Us

  1. Home
  2. About

As ZeusMed, we started our journey with the Metropol Medical Center opened in 2005 in Bahcelievler district of Istanbul..

Since the day we were founded, we continue to provide services by aiming to be one of the reference institutions with our determination to increase patient satisfaction, based on patient and community health, and continue to grow with a quality service approach.

With the motto "There is health in ZeusMed", we offer curative and preventive health services in a friendly and reliable way without compromising scientific ethical principles. As Metropol Health Group, considering the population, socio-cultural distribution and easy transportation opportunities of our region, İzmir's Karşıyaka, Balçova, Hatay, Şemikler, Aliağa and Alsancak  We continue to serve in the regions.

As a health institution that follows and applies advanced technology in its field, we have adopted the principle of providing patient-oriented service in our 7 centers located in Izmir with our expert physician staff and professional team. In all our centers, everything has been considered to ensure that patients receive the most comfortable service from the first moment they are examined to the time they are discharged.

To bring people back to health and keep them healthy, to meet the satisfaction of our patients who expect quality healthcare services, correct diagnosis-correct treatment  To provide health services with an approach without compromising ethical values; With the awareness of social responsibility, we aim to contribute to the society's efforts to increase the health level and quality of life. We work for your health as one of the most established health institutions in Izmir, with our innovative and creative identity, which closely follows the developments in the world in the field of health, and with our friendly employees who are experts in their fields, who value people, and who are constantly supported by in-house training.
