Nutrition and Diet

The Department of Nutrition and Diet provides services on individual diet programs and nutrition education.

Nutrition; It is the use of nutrients for growth, maintenance of life and protection of health. In the prevention of many diseases, during treatment and after the disease, it is necessary to determine the nutritional status of the person and follow up with a personalized, adequate and balanced diet.

All inpatients in our hospital receive meal service planned by our Specialist Dietitian in accordance with a healthy nutrition program.

Successful studies with obesity surgery are continuing on obesity diseases.
